Understanding Humanity Protocol

Humanity Protocol is a decentralized identity verification system that utilizes palm recognition technology to establish Proof of Humanity (PoH) within Web3 applications. This innovative approach allows users to prove their human identity without compromising personal data, addressing critical challenges in online security and privacy.

The Core Technology: Palm Recognition

Unlike traditional biometric systems that may rely on facial recognition or iris scans, Humanity Protocol employs palm recognition technology. This method is considered less invasive and can be easily accessed through smartphones, making it a user-friendly solution for identity verification. Palm vein patterns are unique to each individual and nearly impossible to replicate, providing a robust mechanism for fraud prevention.

Sybil Resistance and Decentralization

A significant challenge in digital communities is Sybil attacks, where a single entity creates multiple fake identities to manipulate or disrupt network operations. Humanity Protocol addresses this by ensuring that each digital identity corresponds to a real human, effectively preventing such attacks. This Sybil-resistant framework is crucial for maintaining the integrity and trustworthiness of decentralized platforms.

The Humanity Protocol Testnet

To refine and perfect its system, Humanity Protocol has launched a testnet, rolled out in three distinct phases:

Phase 1: Human ID Reservation

The initial phase allows users to reserve their unique Human ID, serving as a permanent identifier within the Humanity Protocol ecosystem. Users can secure their Human ID through various methods, including Email, Telegram, X, Discord, Google, and MetaMask. Upon registration, participants receive a referral link to invite others, unlocking potential rewards and airdrops in subsequent phases.

Phase 2: Palm Print Registration via the App

In the second phase, Humanity Protocol introduces a mobile application enabling users to register their palm prints. This biometric data enhances the security and authenticity of the Human ID, ensuring that each identity is unique and verifiable. The app is designed with privacy in mind, utilizing zero-knowledge proofs to protect user data.

Phase 3: Palm Vein Scan on Humanity Devices

The final phase involves scanning users' palm veins using Humanity Protocol's specialized devices. Palm vein patterns offer an additional layer of security due to their complexity and uniqueness. Completing this phase finalizes the identity verification process, granting users full access to the ecosystem's features and rewards.

Participating in the Humanity Protocol Airdrop

Humanity Protocol offers participants the opportunity to earn rewards through its airdrop program. Referrers earn a percentage of the rewards generated by their referrals, creating a perpetuating incentive structure.
